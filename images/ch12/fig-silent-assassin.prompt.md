# 12.3 The Silent Assassin

- Class: Spot illustration
- Priority: Optional
- Placement: Ch 12, near the chapter opening
- Aspect: Small square (1:1)
- Print: pure B&W

## Alt text
A tiny ninja formed from the typo £1,00.00 perches on an invoice corner with a dagger while the office works on, oblivious.

## Prompt
Hand-drawn black-and-white editorial cartoon illustration. Fine ink pen linework with dense crosshatching and stipple shading, on a plain pure-white background. Bold hand-lettered labels in clean marker capitals sit INSIDE the drawing, on the objects themselves. One physical metaphor, played completely straight; wry, deadpan humour. No colour, no border, no title or caption text outside the artwork, no photorealism, no digital gradients.

Format: Landscape, 4:3.

Scene: A small spot illustration. The corner of a large invoice document on a desk. Perched on its edge, a tiny ninja in full black garb with a drawn dagger — and on close inspection the ninja's body is formed from the printed characters of a typo: £1,00.00. In the soft background, out of focus, office workers type on, entirely oblivious. The invoice's other fields and rows are wavy scribble lines only — no readable words.

Lettering: the ONLY readable text in the image is INVOICE at the top of the document, TOTAL DUE with the amount £1,00.00 beside it, and the £1,00.00 characters forming the ninja's body. Everything else on the invoice is wavy scribble lines. No other text of any kind
