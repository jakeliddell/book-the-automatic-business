# 4.1 The Fashionable Coat

- Class: Cartoon (unnumbered)
- Priority: Core — chapter thesis; style-test image
- Placement: Ch 4, at 'a plain automation job wearing a fashionable coat'
- Aspect: Wide landscape (4:3)
- Print: pure B&W

## Alt text
Ordinary pipework on a sales plinth dressed in a trench coat and sunglasses with an AI badge and a x10 price tag; a child points and says it is plumbing.

## Prompt
Hand-drawn black-and-white editorial cartoon illustration. Fine ink pen linework with dense crosshatching and stipple shading, on a plain pure-white background. Bold hand-lettered labels in clean marker capitals sit INSIDE the drawing, on the objects themselves. One physical metaphor, played completely straight; wry, deadpan humour. No colour, no border, no title or caption text outside the artwork, no photorealism, no digital gradients.

Format: Landscape, 4:3.

Scene: A display plinth under a spotlight. The plinth face carries an engraved sign reading THE FUTURE OF BUSINESS (no other text on the plinth). Standing on the plinth: a completely ordinary length of household pipework with a simple valve wheel — but dressed in a stylish belted trench coat and dark sunglasses, wearing a conference lanyard. A large sales tag hangs from the coat. A small crowd of impressed business people admires it. At the front, one small child points at it, unimpressed, with a speech bubble.

Lettering (must appear exactly as written, hand-lettered inside the drawing): AI on the lanyard badge; ×10 on the price tag; THE FUTURE OF BUSINESS on the plinth; speech bubble reading IT'S PLUMBING. No other words anywhere in the image.
