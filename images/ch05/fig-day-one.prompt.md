# 5.1 Day One

- Class: Cartoon (unnumbered)
- Priority: Core
- Placement: Ch 5, at 'arrives brilliant, and completely blank about you'
- Aspect: Wide landscape (4:3)
- Print: pure B&W

## Alt text
A new recruit with an enormous head full of library shelves sits at a first-day desk; the filing cabinet labelled YOUR BUSINESS is open and empty except for a moth.

## Prompt
Hand-drawn black-and-white editorial cartoon illustration. Fine ink pen linework with dense crosshatching and stipple shading, on a plain pure-white background. Bold hand-lettered labels in clean marker capitals sit INSIDE the drawing, on the objects themselves. One physical metaphor, played completely straight; wry, deadpan humour. No colour, no border, no title or caption text outside the artwork, no photorealism, no digital gradients.

Format: Landscape, 4:3.

Scene: A first-day-at-work desk scene. A keen young recruit sits upright in a chair, hands folded. Their head is enormous and drawn in cutaway, revealing an endless library inside: shelves of books receding to a tiny horizon, ladders, stacked volumes. On the wall behind, framed certificates. On the desk in front of the recruit: a small filing cabinet with its drawer pulled fully open — completely empty, with a single moth flying out of it.

Lettering (must appear exactly as written, hand-lettered inside the drawing): READ THE ENTIRE INTERNET on the largest framed certificate; YOUR BUSINESS on the filing cabinet drawer
