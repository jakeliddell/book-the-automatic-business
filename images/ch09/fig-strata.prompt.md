## Prompt
Hand-drawn black-and-white editorial cartoon illustration. Fine ink pen linework with dense crosshatching and stipple shading, on a plain pure-white background. Bold hand-lettered labels in clean marker capitals sit INSIDE the drawing, on the objects themselves. One physical metaphor, played completely straight; wry, deadpan humour. No colour, no border, no title or caption text outside the artwork, no photorealism, no digital gradients. Keep the layout clear and instructional: the drawing must read as a diagram at a glance.

Format: Landscape, 4:3.

Scene: A geological cross-section diagram of the ground, three distinct strata. Bottom stratum, the bedrock: embedded like fossils, small envelopes, cassette tapes of recorded calls, documents, speech bubbles — labelled RAW SOURCES. Middle stratum, a rich glowing seam of neatly organised pages and index cards — labelled THE KEYSTONE — where a small librarian robot with a miner's head-lamp tends the pages with a feather duster. Top: the ground surface, where three little well-heads with buckets on ropes draw up from the seam; at the wells stand a human employee, a friendly AI robot, and a small mechanical automaton, each hauling up a bucket of pages.

Lettering (must appear exactly as written, hand-lettered inside the drawing): RAW SOURCES on the bedrock; THE KEYSTONE on the seam; TEAM, AI, AUTOMATIONS beside the three wells
