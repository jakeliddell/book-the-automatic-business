# 13.1 The Message That Didn't Stop

- Class: Cartoon (unnumbered)
- Priority: Core
- Placement: Ch 13, at the golden rule
- Aspect: Wide landscape (4:3)
- Print: pure B&W

## Alt text
A robot postman proudly delivers a letter asking DID YOU GET OUR PROPOSAL? to a client who is mid-handshake over a signed contract with champagne open.

## Prompt
Hand-drawn black-and-white editorial cartoon illustration. Fine ink pen linework with dense crosshatching and stipple shading, on a plain pure-white background. Bold hand-lettered labels in clean marker capitals sit INSIDE the drawing, on the objects themselves. One physical metaphor, played completely straight; wry, deadpan humour. No colour, no border, no title or caption text outside the artwork, no photorealism, no digital gradients.

Format: Landscape, 4:3.

Scene: An office celebration frozen mid-moment: a client and the business owner shaking hands across a table, a signed contract with a big signature and a SIGNED stamp on the table, an open champagne bottle and two glasses. Into the scene marches a gleaming robot postman with a satchel, proudly holding out a letter to the client. Everyone is frozen, eyes on the letter. The robot beams with pride.

Lettering (must appear exactly as written, hand-lettered inside the drawing): SIGNED stamped on the contract; the offered letter reading DID YOU GET OUR PROPOSAL?
