# 14.2 The Team Photo

- Class: Cartoon (unnumbered)
- Priority: Core — replaces old fig-digital-team
- Placement: Ch 14, at the blended team
- Aspect: Wide landscape (3:2)
- Print: pure B&W

## Alt text
A staff photo wall with humans and robots in identical frames; a robot holds EMPLOYEE OF THE MONTH and the owner's frame at the top reads DIRECTOR.

## Prompt
Hand-drawn black-and-white editorial cartoon illustration. Fine ink pen linework with dense crosshatching and stipple shading, on a plain pure-white background. Bold hand-lettered labels in clean marker capitals sit INSIDE the drawing, on the objects themselves. One physical metaphor, played completely straight; wry, deadpan humour. No colour, no border, no title or caption text outside the artwork, no photorealism, no digital gradients.

Format: Landscape, 4:3.

Scene: An office wall of identical framed staff portraits arranged as an org chart. The frames interleave smiling human employees and robots posed identically — hand on chin, slight smile. One robot's frame carries a small brass plaque and it holds a little trophy: EMPLOYEE OF THE MONTH. One frame contains not a figure but a small machine, its nameplate reading NEW ENQUIRY: LOG, CONFIRM, ALERT. At the top of the chart, the owner's larger frame, its nameplate freshly relabelled — a strip of tape over the old title — reading DIRECTOR.

Lettering (must appear exactly as written, hand-lettered inside the drawing): EMPLOYEE OF THE MONTH on the plaque; NEW ENQUIRY: LOG, CONFIRM, ALERT and DIRECTOR on the nameplates
