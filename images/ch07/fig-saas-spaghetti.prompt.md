# 7.1 SaaS Spaghetti

- Class: Cartoon (unnumbered)
- Priority: Core — signature image; style-test image
- Placement: Ch 7, at the 'SaaS Spaghetti' heading
- Aspect: Wide landscape (4:3)
- Print: pure B&W

## Alt text
A restaurant plate of tangled spaghetti with app windows caught in the strands, each with a monthly price tag; the owner sits before it, napkin tucked in, horrified.

## Prompt
Hand-drawn black-and-white editorial cartoon illustration. Fine ink pen linework with dense crosshatching and stipple shading, on a plain pure-white background. Bold hand-lettered labels in clean marker capitals sit INSIDE the drawing, on the objects themselves. One physical metaphor, played completely straight; wry, deadpan humour. No colour, no border, no title or caption text outside the artwork, no photorealism, no digital gradients.

Format: Landscape, 4:3.

Scene: A restaurant table scene. On a large dinner plate: an enormous tangle of spaghetti, and caught in the strands like meatballs, a dozen small software app windows — little rectangles with title bars — labelled MAILCHIMP, CRM, CALENDLY, ADS, WORDPRESS, THE SPREADSHEET and others. From every strand dangles a small price tag reading £/MO. The business owner sits at the table, napkin tucked into collar, knife and fork raised, faintly horrified. A long bill lies beside the plate.

Lettering (must appear exactly as written, hand-lettered inside the drawing): app names on the windows; £/MO on the tags; the bill reading 14 ITEMS. SERVICE NOT INCLUDED.
