# 7.2 Four Blocks

- Class: Diagram (numbered figure)
- Priority: Core — redraw of old fig-four-blocks
- Placement: Ch 7, at the four-blocks model
- Aspect: Wide landscape (3:2)
- Print: pure B&W

## Alt text
Four solid wooden toy blocks labelled with the four business functions, with a blanket labelled KNOWLEDGE & REPORTING draped across all four.

## Prompt
Hand-drawn black-and-white editorial cartoon illustration. Fine ink pen linework with dense crosshatching and stipple shading, on a plain pure-white background. Bold hand-lettered labels in clean marker capitals sit INSIDE the drawing, on the objects themselves. One physical metaphor, played completely straight; wry, deadpan humour. No colour, no border, no title or caption text outside the artwork, no photorealism, no digital gradients. Keep the layout clear and instructional: the drawing must read as a diagram at a glance.

Format: Landscape, 4:3.

Scene: Four solid wooden toy building blocks in a row on a plain surface, each face carved with its label: FINANCE, ACQUISITION & CONTROL, DELIVERY, TASK. Draped softly across the tops of all four blocks, like a blanket over furniture, a single cloth layer with its label embroidered along the edge. The cloth visibly touches all four blocks.

Lettering (must appear exactly as written, hand-lettered inside the drawing): FINANCE, ACQUISITION & CONTROL, DELIVERY, TASK on the blocks; KNOWLEDGE & REPORTING along the cloth edge
