# 19.1 Everyone Gets a Medal

- Class: Cartoon (unnumbered)
- Priority: Core
- Placement: Ch 19, at 'if it is all first, there is no first at all'
- Aspect: Wide landscape (3:2)
- Print: pure B&W

## Alt text
An Olympic podium with fourteen first-place steps, every task wearing a gold medal and an URGENT sash, all shouting, while the owner holds a single trophy.

## Prompt
Hand-drawn black-and-white editorial cartoon illustration. Fine ink pen linework with dense crosshatching and stipple shading, on a plain pure-white background. Bold hand-lettered labels in clean marker capitals sit INSIDE the drawing, on the objects themselves. One physical metaphor, played completely straight; wry, deadpan humour. No colour, no border, no title or caption text outside the artwork, no photorealism, no digital gradients.

Format: Landscape, 4:3.

Scene: An Olympic-style podium — but with fourteen steps, all exactly the same height, every single one carved with the number 1. On each step stands an anthropomorphised task — an invoice, an email, a phone message, a report, a to-do note — every one wearing a gold medal and a beauty-pageant sash reading URGENT, all shouting and waving their arms for attention. In front of the podium, a single judge — the business owner — holds one solitary trophy, looking from face to face, unable to award it.

Lettering (must appear exactly as written, hand-lettered inside the drawing): the number 1 on every podium step; URGENT on the sashes
