# 17.3 No Front Door

- Class: Cartoon (unnumbered)
- Priority: Optional
- Placement: Ch 17, at 'a shop with no front door'
- Aspect: Wide landscape (3:2)
- Print: pure B&W

## Alt text
A handsome shop with a big window and a CONTACT US sign — and no door anywhere; customers bounce off the glass and leave their money on the pavement.

## Prompt
Hand-drawn black-and-white editorial cartoon illustration. Fine ink pen linework with dense crosshatching and stipple shading, on a plain pure-white background. Bold hand-lettered labels in clean marker capitals sit INSIDE the drawing, on the objects themselves. One physical metaphor, played completely straight; wry, deadpan humour. No colour, no border, no title or caption text outside the artwork, no photorealism, no digital gradients.

Format: Landscape, 4:3.

Scene: A handsome high-street shopfront: striped awning, big display window full of appealing goods, elegant signage reading CONTACT US — and, on careful inspection, no door anywhere in the facade, just continuous brick and glass. Several customers: one bouncing gently off the window glass (impact stars), one walking away shrugging, and one politely leaving a small stack of banknotes on the pavement before wandering off.

Lettering (must appear exactly as written, hand-lettered inside the drawing): CONTACT US on the shop sign
