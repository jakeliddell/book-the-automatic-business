# 18.2 The Receipt

- Class: Cartoon (unnumbered)
- Priority: Core — replaces old fig-your-week
- Placement: Ch 18, at the reclaimed-hours tally
- Aspect: Portrait or square (4:5)
- Print: pure B&W

## Alt text
A till receipt unrolls to the floor itemising reclaimed hours, totalling 700+ hours a year, with the final line CHANGE GIVEN: YOUR EVENINGS.

## Prompt
Hand-drawn black-and-white editorial cartoon illustration. Fine ink pen linework with dense crosshatching and stipple shading, on a plain pure-white background. Bold hand-lettered labels in clean marker capitals sit INSIDE the drawing, on the objects themselves. One physical metaphor, played completely straight; wry, deadpan humour. No colour, no border, no title or caption text outside the artwork, no photorealism, no digital gradients.

Format: Landscape, 4:3.

Scene: A till receipt held at the top by the owner's hand, unrolling extravagantly all the way to the floor and curling at the bottom. The printed lines are legible, itemised like shopping: FOLLOW-UPS ... 260 HRS/YR; SCHEDULING ... 104 HRS/YR; DATA ENTRY ... 156 HRS/YR; CHASING INVOICES ... 78 HRS/YR; REMEMBERING THINGS ... 120 HRS/YR; then a bold ruled total line: TOTAL RECLAIMED: 700+ HRS. At the very bottom, smaller: CHANGE GIVEN: YOUR EVENINGS.

Lettering (must appear exactly as written, hand-lettered inside the drawing): the receipt lines exactly as given
