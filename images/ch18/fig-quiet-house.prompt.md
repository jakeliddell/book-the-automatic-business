# 18.1 The Quiet House

- Class: Cartoon (unnumbered)
- Priority: Core
- Placement: Ch 18, at 'the first morning of school'
- Aspect: Wide landscape (4:3)
- Print: pure B&W

## Alt text
The owner stands in a suddenly calm office, coffee in hand, unsure what to do with the other hand, as a tumbleweed of old sticky notes rolls past and the machine hums outside the window.

## Prompt
Hand-drawn black-and-white editorial cartoon illustration. Fine ink pen linework with dense crosshatching and stipple shading, on a plain pure-white background. Bold hand-lettered labels in clean marker capitals sit INSIDE the drawing, on the objects themselves. One physical metaphor, played completely straight; wry, deadpan humour. No colour, no border, no title or caption text outside the artwork, no photorealism, no digital gradients.

Format: Landscape, 4:3.

Scene: A calm, tidy office interior, morning light through the window. The business owner stands in the middle of the floor holding a coffee cup, the other hand hovering awkwardly with nothing to do. Everything hums quietly: a small dashboard on the wall all in the green, an in-tray empty, a telephone silent. Rolling past his feet: a tumbleweed made entirely of crumpled old sticky notes. Through the window, machinery and little robots visibly getting on with the work.

Lettering (must appear exactly as written, hand-lettered inside the drawing): optionally IN TRAY: 0 on the empty tray
