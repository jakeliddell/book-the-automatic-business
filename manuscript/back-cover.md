# Back Cover {.unnumbered}

**You didn't start your business to end up trapped inside it.**

But somewhere along the way, it happened. Your team checks everything with you. The knowledge lives in your head. Nothing important moves unless you move it. You have become the bottleneck in the business you built, and working harder only pulls the knot tighter.

*The Automatic Business* is the way out.

Written for the owner of a small service firm with a team, it cuts through the noise, and the AI hype, to show you, plainly and without jargon, how to build a business that runs without you in the middle of it.

You will learn:

- The simple triage that decides, for any task, what belongs to a human, what to automation, and what to AI, and why most "AI problems" are really automation you have needed for a decade.
- How to build your Keystone: the living memory that finally gets your business out of your head, and ends key-person dependency for good.
- How to connect your tools, systemise your delivery, and put a team of digital teammates to work, with no code, no developer, and no computer science degree.
- How to win back your time, and end up with a business that could one day run, and even sell, without you.

This is not theory. Jake Liddell has spent more than thirty-five years building systems that take work off people's plates, from the mainframes of the early days to the AI of today, half of it inside the largest companies in the world and half of it for businesses exactly like yours.

Stop being the engine. Become the architect.

**Do what only you can do.**
