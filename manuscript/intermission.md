# A Quick Favour {.unnumbered}

A short interruption, and then we will get straight back to it.

You are about halfway through, and you have just reached the heart of the book: the triage, and the real difference between automation and AI. If any of it has already made you stop and think "that is exactly my business," then I am going to ask you for sixty seconds.

Would you leave an honest review?

I know that nobody much enjoys being asked. But here is the thing. You are not unusual. Somewhere out there is another owner in exactly the spot you are in now: brilliant at the work, buried in the business, quietly certain there must be a better way but with no idea where to start. Right now they are deciding whether this book is worth their time. Your few honest words are what tells them. A line from one real owner does more than anything I could ever write about myself.

It costs nothing, and it takes a minute:

- Reading on Kindle? Swipe to the very last page and the review prompt appears on its own.
- Listening on Audible? Tap the three dots in the corner and choose Rate and Review.
- Reading in print, or anywhere else? Search for *The Automatic Business* on Amazon and scroll down to "Write a review."

Whatever you can manage, even a single line, genuinely helps. Thank you for it.

Now, back to it. Part Three is where we start to build.
